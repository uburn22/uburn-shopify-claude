## Group 1: The product

### Q1. What exactly is UBurn?
UBurn is a plant-based powdered drink mix made with **konjac glucomannan**, purple ube, coconut MCT, L-Carnitine, ginger, and acacia fiber. It supports natural satiety as part of a balanced diet.*

### Q2. Is there caffeine in UBurn?
No — **caffeine-free**. UBurn can be taken any time of day, including in the evening, without disrupting your sleep. It's one of our core differences vs. stimulant-based solutions.

### Q3. How many calories per scoop?
**30 calories** per scoop. No added sugar, no artificial sweeteners. Compatible with most diets.

### Q4. Is UBurn vegan?
Yes, UBurn is **100% plant-based**. All ingredients are vegan.

### Q5. Any allergens?
UBurn is produced in a facility that handles **dairy, gluten, soy, and tree nuts**. Please consult your doctor if you have a known allergy.

## Group 2: How to use

### Q6. How do I take UBurn?
Mix **1 scoop (8g) in 8-10 oz of water**, 30 minutes before a meal or whenever cravings hit (typically around 4PM). The research-backed* protocol requires 3g of glucomannan with 1-2 glasses of water.

### Q7. Can I take it in the evening?
Yes — **no problem at all**. Caffeine-free means UBurn can be taken any time, including before dinner. Many customers report best results when taken before dinner.*

### Q8. How quickly will I see results?
The fullness effect is **immediate from the first scoop**. Most customers report a noticeable shift in cravings and routine after **7 to 14 days** of consistent use.*

### Q9. Can I mix it with something other than water?
Yes, with plant-based milk or a smoothie. Avoid sugary juices which work against the natural effect on blood sugar. Water remains the best option to activate glucomannan.
