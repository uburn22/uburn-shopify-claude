## 1. Konjac · Glucomannan (HERO)
- **Latin** : amorphophallus konjac
- **Benefit** : 4-hour satiety
- **Description (EN)** : Forms a natural gel in the stomach. Research-backed fiber. Clinically validated satiety from the first dose. EFSA ID 3120 — 3g validated daily dose.
- **Tags** : EFSA-backed · Glucomannan 3g/scoop · Soluble fiber
- **Image** : `/images/ingredient/ingredient-konjac-glucomannan.jpg`

## 2. Ube · Purple yam
- **Latin** : dioscorea alata · southeast asia
- **Benefit** : Natural antioxidants
- **Description (EN)** : Purple root from Southeast Asia. Naturally rich in anthocyanins. Source of the signature violet color (no artificial coloring).
- **Tags** : Antioxidants · Anthocyanins · Plant-based
- **Image** : `/images/ingredient/ingredient-ube-purple-yam.jpg`

## 3. Coconut MCT
- **Latin** : medium-chain triglycerides
- **Benefit** : Stable energy
- **Description (EN)** : Coconut-derived MCTs for steady energy without spikes or crashes. Caffeine-free alternative to stimulant-based solutions.
- **Tags** : Caffeine-free · Steady energy · MCT C8/C10
- **Image** : `/images/ingredient/ingredient-coconut-mct.jpg`

## 4. L-Carnitine
- **Latin** : natural amino acid (L-carnitine tartrate)
- **Benefit** : Active metabolism support
- **Description (EN)** : Natural amino acid that supports fat metabolism as part of an active lifestyle.* 400mg per scoop.
- **Tags** : 400mg · Active metabolism · Amino acid
- **Image** : `/images/ingredient/ingredient-l-carnitine.jpg`

## 5. Purple ginger
- **Latin** : zingiber zerumbet
- **Benefit** : Digestive comfort
- **Description (EN)** : Organic ginger root for daily digestive comfort. Supports balanced digestion alongside acacia fiber.
- **Tags** : Digestive comfort · Organic · Anti-inflammatory

- **Latin** : acacia senegal · prebiotic
- **Benefit** : Balanced microbiome
- **Description (EN)** : Prebiotic acacia fiber feeds the good bacteria in the gut. Supports balanced microbiome as part of a varied diet.
