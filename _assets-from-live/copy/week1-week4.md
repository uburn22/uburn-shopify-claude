## Compliance flags
- ❌ **"6 lbs released over 4 weeks"** is a weight loss claim. UBurn cannot legally make weight loss claims as a dietary supplement in the US (FTC requires substantiation by clinical trial). DROP this bullet for US.
- ❌ Avoid "pounds lost" / "weight loss" / "slim" anywhere on US site.
- ✅ "No more 4PM cravings" / "smoother digestion" / "calmer mood" / "no afternoon crash" — all OK as structure/function claims (FDA-allowed for supplements when truthful).
- ✅ Always pair with disclaimer : "*Individual results may vary. These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA."
